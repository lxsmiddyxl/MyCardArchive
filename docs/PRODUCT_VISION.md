# Product vision — MyCardArchive

MyCardArchive is a Pokémon TCG–first collection workspace: **cards**, **sets**, **binders**, **decks**, and **trades**, with scanning and grading helpers that respect real-world collecting workflows.

## Principles

1. **Trust the collection.** Data ownership, RLS-backed access, and clear visibility rules are non-negotiable.
2. **Speed where it matters.** Lists virtualize; hot paths batch and parallelize safely.
3. **Honest UX.** Loading, empty, and error states explain what happened and what to do next.
4. **One design language.** Token-backed surfaces (`mca-ui`) keep binders, decks, trades, and matching feeling like one product.

## North star

Help collectors **organize**, **build**, and **exchange** with confidence—whether they are cataloging a binder, tuning a deck list, or negotiating a trade.

## Non-goals

- Replacing marketplace checkout or payment rails.
- Generic “any TCG” parity before Pokémon flows are excellent.
