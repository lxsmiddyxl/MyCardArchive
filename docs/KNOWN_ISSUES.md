# Known issues (beta)

- **Realtime:** Rare cold starts can delay live trade updates by a few seconds; the status strip may show a reconnect state.
- **Scan:** Low-light or blurry photos may need a manual name or number fix before adding to a binder.
- **Matching:** Discovery ranks depend on your want/have index staying in sync—large imports may take a moment to reflect.
- **Grading:** Visual grading is assistive; final condition judgment is always yours.

Report regressions via **Report an issue** in the account menu (`/support`).
